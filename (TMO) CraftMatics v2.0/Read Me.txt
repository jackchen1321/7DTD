Greetings Survivors,
I hope this mod finds you well. It is a simple ServerSide modlet that allows schematics to be crafted from existing mods that you find in the game. This is great for those
friends who play with you, or join late that might not have that schematic to give them the competitive advantage. 

64 Schematics including 2-3 Food ones are craftable.
